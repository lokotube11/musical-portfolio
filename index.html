Specializations

What I do

01

Orchestral

Writing for strings, brass, woodwinds and percussion — from small chamber pieces to full arrangements. The orchestra as the most direct path to emotion.

02

Atmosphere

Music that sets a mood without demanding attention. Textures and layers that make a moment feel real, whether for a scene, a game, or a place.

03

Games & Film

Music built to serve a story — adaptive, loopable, or timed to picture. Familiar with what scoring for interactive and linear media each demand.

04

Always exploring

No genre is off limits. The goal is always to find what a piece needs — whether that leads somewhere familiar or somewhere new.
